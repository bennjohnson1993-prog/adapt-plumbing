Bathroom and kitchen plumbing for new builds, renovations, and fitouts.

Renovation plumbing that doesn't hold up the build

Renovating a bathroom or kitchen is a big investment. The plumbing needs to be right from the start — because once the tiles are down and the cabinets are in, you don't want to be ripping things apart to fix a pipe that was done wrong.

We handle everything from the initial rough-in through to the final fitoff. We work with your builder and other trades to make sure the plumbing is in the right place at the right time. No delays, no rework, no excuses.

Whether it's a simple bathroom refresh or a full gut-and-rebuild, we've got the experience to get it done properly.

FAQ

Common questions

Do you do full bathroom renovations?

We handle all the plumbing side of bathroom renovations — rough-in, drainage, water supply, and fitoff of fixtures. We work alongside your builder, tiler, and other trades to make sure the plumbing is done right from the ground up.

Can you relocate plumbing during a kitchen renovation?

Yes. We regularly relocate water supply, waste, and gas lines during kitchen renovations. If you're moving your sink, dishwasher, or gas cooktop, we'll make sure the plumbing is in the right place before the cabinetry goes in.

Do you work with builders and other trades?

Absolutely. We coordinate with builders, tilers, electricians, and cabinetmakers on renovation projects. We show up when we're needed, work to the project timeline, and communicate with your builder directly so nothing holds up the job.
