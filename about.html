About Adapt Plumbing

A local Gold Coast plumbing business built on doing the job right.

Our story

Why we started Adapt

We started Adapt because we were tired of seeing the same problems in the industry. Customers getting quoted one price and charged another. Plumbers not showing up when they said they would. Work that looked fine on the surface but fell apart six months later.

We wanted to build something different. A plumbing business on the Gold Coast where you actually get what you're promised. Where the plumber who answers the phone is the same person who turns up at your door. Where the price you're quoted is the price you pay. We're a small, local team and that's how we like it. It means every job gets our full attention.

What we stand for

Three things that drive every job

Do it right the first time

We'd rather spend an extra thirty minutes getting something perfect than come back next week to fix it. Every job is done to last.

Treat every home like our own

Boot covers go on before we step inside. Drop sheets go down. And when we leave, your place looks better than when we arrived.

No surprises, ever

You'll know the price before we start. If something changes mid-job, we stop and talk to you first. No one likes a bill they weren't expecting.

How we work

Our approach on every job

We follow the same process whether it's a dripping tap or a full renovation. Consistency is how we maintain quality.

  • We diagnose the problem before quoting anything
  • We explain what needs doing in plain English
  • We use quality materials, not the cheapest option
  • We communicate throughout — no going quiet mid-job
  • We test everything before we pack up
  • We follow up to make sure it's still right
