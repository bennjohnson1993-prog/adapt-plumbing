FAQ

Common questions

Do I need a licensed gas fitter on the Gold Coast?

Yes. All gas work in Queensland must be carried out by a licensed gas fitter. It's a legal requirement and a safety one. We're fully licensed and issue compliance certificates for every gas job.

Can you connect my gas cooktop or BBQ?

Yes. We connect all types of gas appliances including cooktops, ovens, BBQs, gas heaters, and hot water systems. We'll test for leaks and issue a compliance certificate after every connection.

How do I know if I have a gas leak?

Common signs include a rotten egg smell, hissing sounds near gas lines, dead vegetation near gas pipes, or a higher than normal gas bill. If you suspect a gas leak, ventilate the area, don't use any electrical switches, and call us immediately.
