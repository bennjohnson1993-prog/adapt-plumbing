FAQ

Common questions

How long does a hot water system last?

Most hot water systems last 8-12 years depending on the type, water quality, and maintenance. If yours is over 10 years old and starting to play up, it's usually more cost-effective to replace it than keep repairing it.

Do you install gas hot water systems?

Yes. We supply and install all types of hot water systems including gas, electric, solar, and heat pump. We're licensed gas fitters so we handle the full installation including gas connections and compliance certificates.

My hot water has stopped working. How quickly can you get here?

We understand no hot water is urgent. During business hours (Mon-Fri 6:30am-4:30pm), we aim to get to you the same day or next business day. Call us and we'll give you an honest timeframe.
