The heavy lifting side of plumbing

Not every plumbing job happens inside the house. Pipe and civil work is the infrastructure side — sewer connections, stormwater drainage, water mains, and the underground pipes that make everything else possible.

We handle pipe laying for new builds, subdivisions, and existing properties that need new connections or upgrades. We work with councils and water authorities to make sure everything is approved, installed to spec, and passes inspection first time.

From a single residential sewer connection to larger commercial drainage projects, we've got the equipment and the experience to get it done.

FAQ

Common questions

Do you do residential pipe laying on the Gold Coast?

Yes. We handle both residential and commercial pipe laying including sewer connections, stormwater drainage, and water main connections. Whether it's a new home or an existing property, we can lay new pipes to the required standard.

Can you connect to the council sewer main?

Yes. We're licensed to carry out sewer main connections on the Gold Coast. We handle the approvals process with the local council and water authority, dig the trench, lay the pipe, connect to the main, and backfill. Full compliance.

What's involved in stormwater drainage work?

Stormwater work involves installing or repairing the pipes and pits that carry rainwater away from your property. This includes downpipe connections, stormwater pits, underground drainage pipes, and connections to council stormwater infrastructure.
