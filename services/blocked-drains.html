Blocked Drains — Gold Coast & Brisbane

CCTV inspection, jet blasting, and drain clearing. We find the problem and fix it properly.

We find it, we fix it, it stays fixed

A blocked drain isn't just an inconvenience — left alone it can cause serious damage to your property. We don't just clear the blockage and leave. We find out what caused it so it doesn't happen again next month.

We start every job with a CCTV drain camera inspection. This shows us exactly what's going on inside the pipe — tree roots, grease build-up, collapsed sections, foreign objects. Once we know the cause, we use high-pressure jet blasting or mechanical equipment to clear it.

If the pipe itself is damaged, we'll talk you through your repair options. No pressure, no upselling — just honest advice about what actually needs doing.

FAQ

Common questions

How much does it cost to clear a blocked drain on the Gold Coast?

The cost depends on the severity and location of the blockage. We diagnose first with a CCTV camera, then give you a fixed quote before any clearing work begins. No guesswork, no bill shock.

Do you use CCTV drain cameras?

Yes. We use CCTV drain cameras on most blocked drain jobs. It lets us see exactly what's causing the blockage — whether it's tree roots, a collapsed pipe, or a build-up of grease — so we can fix it properly the first time.

What causes blocked drains on the Gold Coast?

The most common causes are tree roots growing into pipes, grease and fat build-up, foreign objects flushed down toilets, and old clay pipes that have cracked or collapsed. The Gold Coast's subtropical climate means tree roots are particularly aggressive.
